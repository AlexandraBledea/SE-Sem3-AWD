Feelings as Emotional States

Happiness

Happiness is a feeling of joy and satisfaction. It happens when something makes us feel good, whether it's spending time with others, achieving something we wanted, or enjoying a fun experience. It makes us feel positive and energized.

Sadness

Sadness is a feeling of sorrow or disappointment, often linked to loss, failure, or unmet expectations. It can bring a sense of heaviness or grief, leading to introspection and a need for comfort or healing.

Anger

Anger is a strong emotion that occurs when we feel something is unfair, frustrating, or threatening. It can make us feel upset, irritated, or even hostile. When angry, we may want to confront the issue, change the situation, or express our frustration to others.

Fear

Fear is a feeling of being scared or anxious, usually when we think something bad might happen. It makes us want to avoid danger or protect ourselves. Fear can be mild, like feeling nervous, or very strong, like feeling terrified.
